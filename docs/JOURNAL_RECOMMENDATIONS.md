## How to pitch (one paragraph for cover letter)

> We present a minimal analytic model of **continuous** conversion of renewed quantum coherence into the ergotropy of a collisional battery tape. A V-type system with a matched-gap exchange collision admits an exact channel and fixed point. We show that the charging power is maximized by **impedance matching** the collisional extraction rate to the coherence regrowth rate, give a closed finite-precession suppression law, and separate total ergotropy from work accessible without a phase reference (including the full three-regime two-copy result). The paper is a companion theory for coherence harvesting, not a claim about restoring collinear transport power.
